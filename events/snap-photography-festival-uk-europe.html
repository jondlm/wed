ABOUT

Snap is a unique festival and creative retreat aimed at wedding and lifestyle photographers. It features an array of inspiring workshops and activities which will inspire you and push you to level up your business.

Let's meet in the woods. We'll stay there in a beautiful 200 acre woodland camp. We'll toast marshmallows, drink tea and sit around the fire. We might retire early for a restorative sleep, or we might party late into the night. We'll take our time, be mindful, make new friends and hang out with old ones. We'll eat delicious, locally produced food. We'll have a digital detox and go to the beach.

We'll listen to aspiring photographers and help them find their way. We'll hear stories from old hands and learn from each other. We'll talk about ways of seeing and the art of wedding and lifestyle photography. We'll explore our inspirations and passions. We'll leave with a hundred new friends.
